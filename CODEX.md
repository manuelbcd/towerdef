- Do NOT sign commits (no GPG commit signing).
- Do NOT add any LLM/AI signature, watermark, or "Generated by" markers in commits, files, or code comments.

- Do not include credentials, secrets, or private tokens in commits.

## Branching and PRs
- Use feature branches named `feat/<short-description>`.
- Open PRs for non-trivial changes; include a short description, screenshots (if UI), and testing steps.
- Keep PRs small and focused when possible.

## Code ownership & reviews
- Assign at least one reviewer for each PR.
- Prefer pair programming for complex engine logic changes.
